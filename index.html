About Our Institution

A premier institution dedicated to academic excellence and innovation

Established 2019

Leading Institute of Higher Education

PAF-IAST is a distinguished institution established through collaboration between Pakistan and Austria, offering world-class education in applied sciences and technology. Our mission is to develop skilled professionals equipped with cutting-edge knowledge and practical expertise.

We pride ourselves on our state-of-the-art facilities, internationally recognized faculty, and strong industry partnerships that provide our students with unparalleled learning opportunities.
